About me

I received a Bachelor of Music Degree from UNC School of the Arts in 2010 and I still enjoy expanding musical skills on the instruments I've taught myself since then. However, my desire to participate in the realm of science and technology eventually pulled me far outside of my original comfort zone.

While working as a bilingual agent in a call center, I saw room to improve the organization and speed with which we accessed information. During a few slow days at work, I began building and sharing tools to make myself and fellow agents better at our jobs. I eventually used a WYSIWYG editor to build a site to keep all of the tools in one place.

Most recently, I took a risk and quit work to attend the Front End Engineering course at The Iron Yard in Durham, NC. It was extremely challenging but with persistence and partnership, I graduated on December 2nd. I currently read or practice coding for about 8 hours each day since, I have no desire to 'coast' or lose these new skills.
