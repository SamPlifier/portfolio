About me

I received a Bachelor of Music Degree from UNC School of the Arts in 2010 and I still enjoy expanding musical skills on the instruments I've taught myself and others since then. However, after being a spectator of the incredible achievements of technology, I felt an increasing desire to get more involved.

While working as a bilingual agent (and eventually supervisor) in a call center, I saw room to improve the organization and speed with which, we accessed information. During a few particularly slow days at work, I began building and sharing tools to make myself and my fellow agents better at our jobs. I eventually used a WYSIWYG editor to build a site to keep my various maps, spreadsheets, payment policies, etc. in one place. During the development and maintenance of the site, I became certain that learning front end web development would be my new path.

In the fall of 2016 I took a risk and quit work to attend the Front End Engineering course offered at The Iron Yard in Durham, NC. It was an extremely challenging curriculum to conquer in 3 months, but, with a combination of persistence, wonderful guidance from instructors, and other motivated students, I graduated on December 2nd. I'm excited to continue furthering my skills independently and at my next place of work.
